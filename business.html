"My business card design project"

Designing my business card, as a part of the brand, is a project that would help me to represent myself and establish contacts within my professional network.


(Please click on the pictures to zoom it in).

A business card is a comfortable tool that helps any specialist to represent itself and leave necessary contact information. At first sight creating a business card is a quite simple task, however my concept of the design and the design approach have proved opposite.

There are so many wonderful creations of business cards. I reviewed some of them and used it as my inspiration.

Business card designs vary according to forms, it's functionalities, types and styles. I decided to chose more classical option, with a fusion of minimalism and modernity.

Before proceeding with the actual design, I researched what technical requirements are necessarily for a business card. The border lines below are under "typographic" group of technical characteristics. Here are the explanations for the main lines. Safety line - all text should be within it's border. Bleed are - all elements that reach the line should extend over it. Trim marks - the lines where a business card will be cut after it is printed.

The next step was sketching and drawing a few options for my future card.

I continue to follow my brand style and thus I used same color palette, typography and the logo.

One of the design solutions for my business card was creating a QR code that would open my website, where users can find my contact information, as well as my design work.

I took a part of the logo - an interpreted letter that looks like a fish, and incorporated it to the interface of my business card. Thus, it supports the brand consistency, as well as gives live vibes to the design.

I plan to use letterpress printing technique, that would cover border of the logo and some of the text.
