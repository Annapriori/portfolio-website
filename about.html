About Me

“A background in International Relations and empathic creativity helps me generate a user-centered design”.

Hello! My name is Anna Eivör. I am Icelandic-Ukrainian UI/UX designer. I am an independent artist, with Master Degree in International Relations and politics, transferred from working in the tourist field into digital art and animation, and then to UI/UX industry. I was always in between the classic art, digital world and structural work. For me UI is the perfection of all of them in one and I am happy to become a professional here. I am a fast-learner, open to new things and experiments, hard-working and creative. I speak a few languages and would love to relocate to work for the company of my dreams!
